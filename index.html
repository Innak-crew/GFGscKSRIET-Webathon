WEBATHON

Event: Web Developer Showcase

Welcome to Webathon, where web developers like you can unleash your creativity and expertise! Join us in this exciting event to showcase your web development projects and compete for recognition and exciting prizes.

Submission Details

Submission Requirements:

  • Submit a screen recording of your website in action, accompanied by an explanation of its features and functionality.
  • Submit the video as a Google Drive link with general access to anyone with the link option enabled.
  • Provide the GitHub repository link containing the original code of your website.
  • Submit an abstract outlining the purpose and design of your website.

Originality:

All submitted websites must be original creations. Plagiarism or copying code from existing sources will result in disqualification.

Judging Criteria:

  • Creativity and Innovation
  • Functionality and User Experience
  • Code Quality and Efficiency
  • Overall Presentation and Explanation

Prizes:

  • First Place: ₹1500
  • Second Place: ₹1000
  • Third Place: ₹500

Important Dates:

  • Winners Announcement: 07/05/2024 (Evening)

Certification:

E-certification will be provided to all participants upon completion of the event.

Rules and Regulations:

  • Participants must adhere to the submission requirements and provide all necessary materials within the specified deadlines.
  • Any form of cheating, including plagiarism or copying code, will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Teams must consist of a maximum of 2 members. Individual participation is also allowed.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable.
  • The decision of the judges is final and binding.
  • By participating in the event, participants agree to abide by all rules and regulations set forth by the organizers.

Get ready to showcase your talent and let your creativity shine! Good luck to all participants!
